Summer Swim Camps

Weekly camp sessions that build skills, confidence and water safety.

Building Swimming Skills All Summer Long

Who Camps Are For

Summer swim camps are designed for children who want to keep improving their swimming during the summer. Camps are a great option for swimmers who already have basic water confidence and are ready to build stronger skills.

What Swimmers Practise

During each camp session, swimmers work with instructors on stroke technique, breathing, starts, turns and safe pool habits. The goal is to help swimmers improve while still enjoying their time in the water.

Why Families Choose Camps

Camps give swimmers regular practice across the week, which helps them build confidence faster. They also give families a structured summer activity that supports fitness, safety and fun.
